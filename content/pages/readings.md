Arbisi-Kelm, Timothy, and Sun-Ah Jun. "A Comparison of Disfluency Patterns in Normal and Stuttered Speech." In _Proceedings of Disfluency in Spontaneous Speech Workshop._ Aix-en Provence, France, 2005.
